Sonnet of Fidelity (Vinicius de Moraes) Of everything, I will be attentive to my love Before, and with such zeal, and always, and so much That even in the face of the greatest charm My thoughts are more enchanted by him. I want to live him in every vain moment And in praise I will spread my song And laugh my laugh and shed my tears To your regret or your joy. And so, when you look for me later Who knows death, anguish of those who live Who knows the loneliness, the end of those who love? I can tell me about the love (that I had): That it is not immortal, since it is flame But let it be infinite while it lasts.
